About HelpKart

We are on a mission to bridge the gap between those who want to help and those in need—through smart, impactful donations.

Our Mission

HelpKart aims to provide essential donation kits to underprivileged communities across India, ensuring timely relief and lasting impact. Our platform makes it easy and transparent for anyone to contribute to meaningful causes.

Our Vision

We envision a future where no one goes without hygiene, food, education, or warmth—because everyone deserves dignity, health, and opportunity.
